# G2 — a=1 Climb Realizability (**COMPLETE**)

**Plan / ledger:** `g2_a1_climb_realizability/IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md`,

**Artifacts:** `g2_a1_climb_realizability/artifacts/`

## Why

G1 P2.1 killed F7-as-exit-tax. G1 P2.2 forced the next object: **every**
record-setting climb step is **a=1**. G2 pressure-tests 2-adic realizability
of pure and mixed climb words.

## Verdict-first

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| V0–V2 | **ALL PASS** |
| Climb runs in sample | **662** runs / 1217 upcrossings; a=1 rate **1.0** |
| Max climb-run L | **20** (Q1.1 CONFIRMED) |
| L histogram geometric (mode L=1)? | **No** — mode L=2 (25,155,141 for L=1,2,3) → Q1.2 **REFUTED** |
| Pure a=1 cylinders L≤24 | **All nonempty**; x≡−1 mod 2^{L+1}; min_x=2^{L+1}−1 → Q2.* **all CONFIRMED** |
| Top-20 gap words realizable? | **20/20** → Q3.1 CONFIRMED |
| Long (≥8) words free of tiny witnesses? | **No** — min_x e.g. 15355, 89083, 154619 <1e6 → Q3.2 **REFUTED** |
| Mixed words stricter mod than pure a=1? | **Yes** (18/18) → Q3.3 CONFIRMED |
| U ≤ M+1 (max_d≥0)? | **Yes** (0 violations / 284 scoped) → Q4.1 CONFIRMED |

**Bottom line:** Pure a=1 climbs are **exactly** the −1 cylinder in ℤ₂
(finite depth: mod 2^{L+1}). That does **not** kill finite climbs — witnesses
exist at every L (Mersenne odds 2^{L+1}−1). Mixed climb-adjacent words from
real orbits are also realizable, often with **stricter** moduli than pure a=1,
but still with **small** positive witnesses for length≥8 frequent words.
Escape is not blocked by “no integer realizes long a=1”; it would need a
stronger composition law (how climbs chain under the true credit word /
deficit).

## Story

1. **V0–V2** — 80049391: 23 ups all a=1; lemma a=1⇔x≡3 mod 4; residue
   a-prediction OK. Receipt: `v0v2_report.json`.
2. **E1** — 308 starts (HR + breach200 + 100 random seed 20260709).
   climb_run = maximal a=1 block with ≥1 upcrossing. L_hist peaks at 2.
   Receipts: `e1_climb_runs.csv`, `e1_summary.json`.
3. **E2** — Closed form pure a=1 through L=32. Inverse limit −1.
   Receipt: `e2_cylinders.csv`.
4. **E3** — Top 20 gap-words; cylinder engine fixed (must realize full
   itinerary, not stack a’s on one x). All nonempty; long words have
   small min_x. Receipt: `e3_summary.json`.
5. **E4** — U≤M+1 on nonnegative max_d starts: holds.

# G3 — Deficit-Compatible Climb Chains (**COMPLETE**)

**Plan / ledger:** `g3_deficit_climb_chains/IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md`, ledger G3-0…G3-1  
**Artifacts:** `g3_deficit_climb_chains/artifacts/`  
**Tests:** `test_g3.py` ALL PASS

## Why
G2 showed pure a=1 cylinders are always realizable (−1 class). Escape needs
the **deficit** side: \(d' = d + c - a\) with Sturmian credit.

## Verdict-first
| Claim | Result |
|-------|--------|
| Ups only at drop phases (c=2)? | **YES — 100%** of 1217 ups (R1.1–R1.3 CONFIRMED) |
| Mersenne a=1 prefixes grow corridor without bound in L? | max_d_prefix ≤ L (R2.2); extended max_d **not** monotone (R2.3); mean ups/L ≈0.507 slightly over 0.50 (R2.1 REFUTED) |
| Forced a=1 for L=24 kills nonneg deficit? | **NO** — 100% of dual-grid cells stay nonneg (R3.* REFUTED); a=1 **inflates** mean d because E[c]≈1.585 |
| Consecutive upcrossing streaks | max=**2**; only ~68% isolated (R4.1 CONFIRMED, R4.2 REFUTED) |

**Bottom line:** Upcrossings are **exactly drop-phase + a=1** in this sample
(structural, not ~90%). Long forced a=1 is *deficit-friendly* on average
(not a kill). What remains hard is chaining **many upcrossings**: streaks
cap at 2 here, and each up needs a drop letter at the ceiling. Escape would
need many drop-aligned a=1 hits without being killed by intervening
support-phase dynamics — the dual game is now sharply posed.

# G4 — Drop-Gated Upcrossings & Streak Bounds (**COMPLETE**)

**Plan / ledger:** `g4_drop_gate_streaks/`  
**Tests:** ALL PASS

## Theorem U (closed)
Every upcrossing satisfies \(d=C\) (at ceiling), \(a=1\), \(c=2\) (drop).
**Proof:** \(d'=d+c-a\); need \(c-a > C-d\ge 0\); max \(c-a=1\) ⇒ slack 0 and \((c,a)=(2,1)\).
Machine-checked: 0 counterexamples on free sample (1180 ups) and odds <1e5 (41209 ups); 72/72 algebra cases.

## Empirical structure
| Fact | Number |
|------|--------|
| Max drop-run in credit (10k & block 53) | **2** |
| Max up-streak (free orbits) | **2** |
| Drop-pair realized as double-up | **5.1%** |
| Post-up is support phase | **74.8%** |
| Support then immediate second up | **0** (corollary of U) |
| Greedy dual ups per 53 steps | **31** (one per drop; S4 soft bound REFUTED) |

## Bottom line
- **Form of climb is solved:** ups = drop ∧ a=1 ∧ ceiling.
- **Streak length** bounded by Sturmian drop-runs (here ≤2).
- **Up count** is *not* capped by streak structure alone — a walker that stays on the ceiling can up on every drop. Escape obstruction must still kill **sustained ceiling residence** (precision/shell / 2-adic a=1 availability), not just streak length.
