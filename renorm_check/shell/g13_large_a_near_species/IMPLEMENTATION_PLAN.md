# G13 — Large-a Drain Near Species

**Prior (G12):** Mersenne x_L always hits species (max 190 steps for L≤40);
steps can be **< L** (species mid-path); steps grow with L (ρ̂≈0.81).
**Prior (species):** S(x)=1 iff a is large enough that 3x+1 is pure 2-power.

**Question:** In free orbits, what is the distribution of \(a\) on the last
1–3 odd-steps before species entry? Is the penultimate step typically large-a?

## Predictions
| ID | Claim | Conf |
|----|-------|------|
| U13.1 | On free starts: last step into species has mean a ≥ 3 | 0.55 |
| U13.2 | ≥50% of last steps have a ≥ 2 | 0.70 |
| U13.3 | Fraction of last steps with a=1 ≤ 0.40 | 0.50 |
| U13.4 | Mean a on last step > mean a on all steps of same trajectories | 0.60 |
